About Open Access Art Bot

Open Access Art Bot is a hobby project, and not a business. Accordingly, we've minimized our data collection and store only basic info about your Slack workspace and anonymized data on search terms and selected artworks.

Why do we store search terms and selected artworks?

Linking search terms to artworks lets us create a provisional library of tags, helping to improve results and to better understand the artworks we make available.

Security

We use SSL encryption and HTTPS for all cross-server communication to make sure your personal data stays personal.
